**Codebook**

This codebook describes the variables used in the course project for Getting and Cleaning Data.

**Input Data**

The original dataset can be found here: http://archive.ics.uci.edu/ml/datasets/Human+Activity+Recognition+Using+Smartphones 

These data are from the accelerometer and gyroscope of Samsung Galaxy S smartphones from users conducting one of six activities: walking, walking upstairs, walking downstairs, sitting, standing or laying.
